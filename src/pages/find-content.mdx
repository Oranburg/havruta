# Find your way in

The point of this app was always to get you to the table. Studying alone keeps you in the daf, and the next steps are a partner, a shiur, and a room. Below are real places to find them. These are independent organizations, listed here to help you take the next step, not run by this app. Details change, so confirm directly, and where a shiur meets in person, call ahead.

## Find a study partner

- <a href="https://www.partnersintorah.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Partners in Torah</a> gives you a free one-to-one study partner by phone or Zoom, with a daily-daf option, and usually matches you within two days. The mentors are traditional, and the program welcomes learners of every background.

## Find a shiur or a minyan near you

- The <a href="https://dafyomidirectory.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Daf Yomi Directory</a>, run by Agudath Israel's Daf Yomi Commission, is a searchable directory of in-person daf yomi shiurim around the world. Call ahead to confirm a shiur is still meeting. For help, write dafyomi@agudah.org.
- <a href="https://mobilegabbai.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MobileGabbai</a> finds minyanim near you within a radius you choose, with directions.

## Learn along with the daf each day

- <a href="https://alldaf.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OU AllDaf</a> offers free daily daf shiurim in audio and video from the Orthodox Union, with an app.
- <a href="https://mdydaf.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MDY, the daily shiur of Reb Eli Stefansky</a>, is a free live shiur on Zoom with a large worldwide following, also available on video and audio.
- <a href="https://www.realcleardaf.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Real Clear Daf</a> gives short, clear daily audio shiurim with printable notes.

## Women's daf

- <a href="https://hadran.org.il/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Hadran</a> is Rabbanit Michelle Farber's daily daf shiur, live on Zoom and as a podcast in Hebrew and English, with a learning community of women at every level. The shiur is taught by a woman, and the community is non-denominational.

## The text itself

- <a href="https://www.sefaria.org/daf-yomi" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Sefaria</a> holds the full Talmud in Hebrew and English with the daily daf, free and open. It is the text this app and many others are built on.

Wherever you are on the way, the aim is the same, to bring you, G-d willing, to a partner across a table.
