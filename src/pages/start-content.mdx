# Start here

You open the app, read today's page, write your own reading, and then study with a partner who challenges what you wrote. That is the whole session. This page walks through each step.

## Open today's daf

The home screen shows you today's page, the same folio every daf yomi learner worldwide is studying today. Tap the daf image or press the read button to open the text and the image of the printed page side by side. Reading the daf is free, with no account and no key required.

There is a read-aloud button that reads the Aramaic and Hebrew transliteration aloud, word by word, with the vowels. Tap it if you want to hear how the words sound before you work through the text. You can toggle the transliteration display on or off at any time in Settings.

## Write your own reading first

Before you engage the partner, write something. A sentence is enough: what you think the passage is doing, where the argument is going, what is puzzling you, what the Mishnah seems to be claiming. It does not need to be correct. The act of committing a reading to words, before anyone pushes back, is where your learning begins. Acting first is how you learn: the challenge lands harder when there is something to challenge.

## The havruta challenges what you wrote

Once you submit your reading, the partner takes it up. It presses on what you wrote, asks whether a particular word supports your reading, or notes where the next passage creates a problem for it. The objection is the center of the method. A partner who only agrees has not done the job.

You answer. The partner presses again. You stay on the passage until you have wrung some resolution from it, even when the resolution is that the question stands open. Everything said in a session saves automatically to your Archive.

## Setting up the partner

The partner runs on an AI model. To use it you need an API key, which you enter once in Settings. There is a free option: Google Gemini offers a free tier that works for this purpose. Go to Settings, choose Gemini as the provider, and paste your key. You will not be charged for ordinary use within the free tier.

If you already have an Anthropic or OpenAI key, those work too. The key is stored only on your device and sent directly to the model provider; the app does not see it.

Once the key is set up the partner is ready. You can study the text and read aloud without any key at all; the key is needed only to engage the havruta.
